Specialist Areas

  • Musculoskeletal (MSK) and Sports Imaging Radiologist: A specialized expertise in the field of musculoskeletal and sports-related imaging, focusing on the diagnosis and treatment of conditions affecting bones, joints, muscles, and soft tissues.
  • MRI Arthrograms: Utilizing magnetic resonance imaging (MRI) technology to perform arthrograms, a diagnostic procedure used to evaluate joint structures and identify issues within joints such as shoulders, hips, wrists, elbows, knees, and ankles.
  • Diagnostic Reporting: Proficient in providing comprehensive and accurate diagnostic reports for various imaging modalities including X-rays, CT scans, and MRI scans, aiding in the precise identification and assessment of medical conditions.
  • Diagnostic MSK Ultrasound Scanning: Skilled in using ultrasound imaging to evaluate and diagnose musculoskeletal conditions, providing detailed visualizations of tendons, ligaments, muscles, and joints.
  • Guided Injections: Experienced in performing ultrasound-guided injections for tendons, joints, and soft tissues, employing precise imaging guidance to administer therapeutic treatments effectively.
  • Image-Guided Steroid Injections: Proficient in performing image-guided steroid injections (cortisone) to alleviate pain and reduce inflammation in affected areas, enhancing patient comfort and facilitating the healing process.
  • Myelographies: Competent in conducting myelographies, a specialized imaging procedure that involves injecting a contrast dye into the spinal canal to assess spinal cord and nerve root conditions.?
  • FNP Thyroids/Aspirations: Skilled in performing fine needle puncture (FNP) procedures for thyroid nodules, aiding in the evaluation and diagnosis of thyroid-related disorders.
  • Cyst Aspirations: Proficient in performing cyst aspirations, a minimally invasive procedure used to drain fluid-filled cysts and alleviate symptoms.
  • CT/Ultrasound Biopsies: Capable of performing biopsies of tumor masses using CT or ultrasound guidance, facilitating accurate sample collection for further diagnostic evaluation.
  • MRI/CT Protocols Modification & Development: Experienced in modifying and developing MRI and CT protocols to optimize imaging parameters and obtain high-quality images, ensuring accurate and detailed diagnostic information.
